Sandy Creeks Resort-An Oasis of Tranquility

Located on the world famous Diani Beach, The Sandy Creeks Beach Resort and Spa is set within 100 acres of tropical gardens, overlooking the Indian Ocean. An oasis of tranquillity and serenity, the Resorts picturesque location is nestled on a coral headland overlooking vast stretches of golden beach. Shaded by indigenous coastal vegetation and exotic coconut palms the resort stretches over 500 metres of golden beach front offering the ultimate luxury beach holiday.

Sandy Creeks Beach Resort and Spa is renowned as Kenya's leading all-inclusive resort and takes pride in an exemplary level of service and attention to detail.
